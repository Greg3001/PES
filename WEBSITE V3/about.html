Our Mission: Breathing Cleaner Seas

Naval Industry Monitoring Systems is a dynamic startup born from a deep commitment to environmental stewardship and the power of technological innovation. Based in Castelldefels, Catalonia, Spain, we are driven by a clear mission: to significantly improve the atmospheric quality around ports and across our seas.

We leverage the vast and insightful data provided by Earth observation satellites to meticulously measure the concentration of various gases emitted by vessels. Our advanced analytics pinpoint the primary sources of pollutants, providing crucial information for targeted interventions and policy adjustments.

A Data-Driven Approach to Sustainability

Our approach is rooted in the precision and scalability of satellite technology. By continuously monitoring maritime regions, we gather comprehensive data on emissions. This data is then processed through our proprietary algorithms to identify trends, detect anomalies, and attribute pollution to specific vessels.

Beyond identification, we are dedicated to empowering the maritime industry to adopt more sustainable practices. Our platform offers valuable insights that enable vessel companies to optimize their routes, reduce fuel consumption, and ultimately minimize their environmental footprint. We believe that economic efficiency and ecological responsibility can go hand in hand.

Envisioning a Greener Future for the Naval Industry

Our vision extends beyond simply monitoring. We aspire to be a catalyst for transformative change within the naval industry. By providing transparent and actionable data, we aim to foster a culture of environmental accountability and drive the adoption of cleaner technologies and operational strategies.

We are committed to continuous innovation, exploring new data sources and refining our analytical capabilities to provide ever more accurate and impactful insights. Collaboration is also central to our vision. We actively seek partnerships with port authorities, shipping companies, research institutions, and regulatory bodies to collectively work towards a healthier planet.
